About me

Who am I?

Hi there! I am Himanshi Mehta, an undergraduate majoring in Data Science at Purdue Univeristy, West Lafayette, USA. I am a transfer student from IIT Bombay, Mumbai, India. I hail from Udaipur (Rajasthan), a city with variegated people, colossal palaces, and brimming lakes. This site list outdated projects. See my CV for the recent ones!

What am I interested in?

Curious by construction, a plethora of topics ranging from discrete math to cognitive psychology interest me. I am enthralled by the amazing world conjured up by the interplay of computer science, education science, and psychology. I sincerely believe that recent technological advances and the ones yet to come have the capacity of changing the face of pedagogy and the society at large.
Are Artificial Intelligence and Technology the Dawn or the Demise? As of now, we aren't in a position to provide a well-founded answer. But as ideas of visionaries like Elon Musk become less science fiction and more grounded in reality day by day, one thing is for certain: AI and Tech definitely make up our future.
This thought drives me to learn and contribute to this BOOM. I am lucky enough to have been born in this perplexing era of computers and artificial intelligence.

What invigorates me?
My favorite leisure activities are badminton and novels. I am a voracious reader of paperbacks and a national-level badminton player. I am fortunate to have found my lifetime passions - badminton and reading at a very young age. Naturally, both were a grand part of my childhood and growing up. While novels of Leo Tolstoy and Harry Potter transported me to a faraway magical world, badminton became my link to the real world. Over the years the struggle of playing competitively in a male-dominated field has helped me realize my feminity and the responsibilities that came with it.

Conclusively, I identify myself as a "thinker" and love science, badminton, and books. I love playing games and conducting "thought experiments" on people's actions and motivations.
I am always up for discussing ideas.
